About

My favourite dish is red sauce pasta. It is made up of tomato sauce, pasta, vegetables like zucchini, corn, broccoli, spinach, bell peppers, tomato, capsicum, carrot and onion. Even cheese and some herbs can be added to it. While we do think of pasta as a culturally Italian food, it is likely the descendent of ancient Asian noodles. A common belief about pasta is that it was brought to Italy from China by Marco Polo during the 13th century. There are many types of pasta's in the world with different shapes, colors and sizes. Some examples are: Ziti, Penne, Rigatoni, Mafaldine ,Butterfly and many more. Pasta can also be made from different grains like wheat, rice, barley and many more. It's a good source of energy and can give you fiber, too, if it's made from whole grain. That can help with stomach problems and may help lower cholesterol.


Recipe

Here is the recipe for pasta:

1. Heat 1 teaspoon of olive oil. Saute bell peppers and onions on a high flame for 2 minutes. You can also skip bell peppers and add onion. The taste would be different but good. Set them aside to cool.

2. To the same pan add ripe tomatoes and saute for 2-3 minutes.

3. Tomatoes must turn soft. Add red chilli flakes as needed. Set this aside to cool.

4. When cooled, add them to a blender jar.

5. Blend to a smooth or coarse paste. We prefer smooth. Set this aside.

6. While you make the sauce, you can bring 2 liters water to a boil in a large pot. Add salt as needed.

7. When the water comes to boil, add pasta. Cook al dente following the instructions on the pack.

8. Drain off in colander and rinse to remove extra starch.

9. Put back the same pan on the stove with another 1 tbsp of oil. When hot, saute garlic and red chilli flakes until fragrant.

10. Add chopped onions.

11. Saute until the raw smell goes away.

12. Add the sauce and salt. Mix and cook on a meduim flame until the sauce thickens.

13. Add oregano. Mix well. Off the stove.

14. Add the cooked pasta. Add another tbsp of of olive oil now.This is optional.

15. Saute until well combined.

16. Add crushed pepper and serve hot.
